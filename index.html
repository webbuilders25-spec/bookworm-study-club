Frequently asked

What are the opening hours? +
We are open 7 AM – 11 PM, Monday to Sunday.
How do I become a member? +
You can register online or visit our front desk.
Is Wi-Fi included? +
Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is free for all members.
Is there parking available? +
Yes, we have a dedicated car park for members.
Is the prayer room open to all? +
Yes, it is available for all members at all times.
Can I bring food inside? +
Food is only allowed in the café area.
Are lockers available? +
Yes, lockers are available on a first-come basis.
